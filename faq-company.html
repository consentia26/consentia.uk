FAQ – Website Creation

Answers to common questions about web development and our services.

How long does it take to build a website?

Timeline depends on complexity. A simple site takes 2-4 weeks, while complex e-commerce sites may take 6-12 weeks. We provide detailed timelines in our quotes.

What is included in your website packages?

Custom design, responsive development, SEO optimization, content integration, testing, and launch. We also offer ongoing support and maintenance.

Do you provide website maintenance?

Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance packages including updates, security, backups, and performance monitoring to keep your site running smoothly.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Absolutely. We can modernize your current site with new design, improved functionality, and better user experience while preserving your content.

What technologies do you use?

We use modern technologies like HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, WordPress, and custom CMS solutions. We choose the best tools for each project.

Do your websites work on mobile devices?

Yes, all our websites are fully responsive and optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices to ensure great user experience across all platforms.

What about SEO and search engine optimization?

We include basic SEO setup in all projects. For advanced optimization, we offer separate SEO packages to improve your search rankings and visibility.

Can you integrate e-commerce functionality?

Yes, we specialize in e-commerce websites with secure payment gateways, inventory management, and shopping cart features using platforms like WooCommerce or custom solutions.

Do you offer domain and hosting services?

We can help with domain registration and recommend reliable hosting providers. We also offer managed hosting solutions for your website.

What if I need changes after the website is launched?

We provide post-launch support and revisions. Our maintenance packages include updates, bug fixes, and content changes.

Can you migrate my existing website to a new platform?

Yes, we handle website migrations carefully, preserving content, SEO rankings, and functionality while moving to better platforms.

What is your process for starting a new project?

We begin with a consultation, then planning, design, development, testing, and launch. We keep you updated throughout the process.

Do you provide training on how to manage the website?

Yes, we offer training sessions to teach you how to update content, manage users, and perform basic maintenance on your new website.

What makes your websites secure?

We implement security best practices including SSL certificates, secure coding, regular updates, and protection against common vulnerabilities.

Can you integrate social media and third-party tools?

Absolutely. We can integrate social media feeds, analytics tools, CRM systems, email marketing, and other third-party services.

What if my project requirements change during development?

We work flexibly. Changes are accommodated within scope, and we discuss any additional costs for significant modifications.
