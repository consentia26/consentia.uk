Recover Hacked Social Media Accounts

Get structured GDPR support to regain access to Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, or other social media accounts.

If your Instagram, Facebook or TikTok account has been hacked, lost access, or someone is impersonating you online, you may be dealing with unlawful use of your personal data.

Under UK GDPR, organisations processing personal data must ensure appropriate security measures (Article 32) and lawful processing (Article 6). Where personal data is misused, individuals may rely on the right to erasure under Article 17.
