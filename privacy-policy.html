PRIVACY & CONFIDENTIALITY

Your Data is Safe With Us

Transparent privacy practices, secure handling, and professional confidentiality standards.

Confidentiality First

Consentia is a website creation consultancy. We respect your privacy and handle your contact and project information with care, security, and professionalism. Your data is never shared without explicit consent or legal obligation.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you contact us or request a quote:

  • Contact details: name, email address, phone number, and business information.
  • Project details: website goals, design preferences, platform requirements, and technical specs.
  • Usage data: analytics and cookies used to improve our website and service experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create proposals, estimates, and project plans.
  • Respond to inquiries, updates, and support requests.
  • Deliver website development, hosting, maintenance, and consultation services.
  • Improve our services, website performance, and customer experience.

Confidentiality and Security

We keep your project information confidential and do not share it with third parties unless required to fulfil your order or by law.

We also work with you to define website privacy, security, and compliance practices that protect your users and brand reputation.

Data Protection Practices

We apply appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure.

This includes secure storage, access controls, and encryption where needed, aligned with current best practices for web services.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies to provide a better browsing experience and to understand how visitors use our content. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the information we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct or update inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal data when it is no longer needed.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.

To exercise your rights, email us at contact@consentia.uk.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
