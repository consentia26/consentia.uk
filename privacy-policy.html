Privacy and Confidentiality

How we protect your personal data and ensure your confidentiality.

At Consentia Data Protection, we take your privacy and confidentiality seriously. We are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us and ensuring it is handled securely, responsibly, and transparently.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information: name, email, phone number, address, and any other details you provide when contacting us.
  • Usage information: how you interact with our website, pages visited, and other analytics data.
  • Professional information: company, role, and any business-related information shared for GDPR consultancy purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our GDPR consultancy services.
  • Respond to your inquiries, requests, and support needs.
  • Send updates, newsletters, or other communications if you have opted in.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Confidentiality

All information shared with us is treated with strict confidentiality. We will never disclose your personal or professional data to third parties without your explicit consent, except where required by law or for legitimate business purposes directly related to your request.

4. Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All data is stored securely in compliance with GDPR requirements.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Object to the processing of your data for marketing purposes.
  • Request data portability.

To exercise your rights, please contact us at consentiagdpr@gmail.com.

6. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy and Confidentiality Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with the date of the latest revision.
