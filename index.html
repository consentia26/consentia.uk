How We Work

Consultation: You describe what you want to achieve with your website creation or adjustment.
Proposal: We provide a detailed consultation and a customised proposal with timeline and pricing.
Design: Our design team creates mockups and wireframes based on your feedback.
Development: We develop your website with modern technologies, responsive design, and optimisation.
Testing: We perform thorough testing to ensure security, performance and cross-device compatibility.
Launch: We launch the website and provide training on how to manage and update content.
Support: Ongoing support and maintenance services keep your site updated, secure, and performing optimally, with data protection and cybersecurity expertise included.
